ABOUT US

Story

At QScript Coffee, our story begins with a passion for exceptional coffee. Founded on the belief that every cup should be an experience, we meticulously source the finest beans and craft each brew with precision and care. Our journey is one of dedication to quality, innovation, and the artistry of coffee-making, creating a space where every sip tells a story of flavor, aroma, and craftsmanship.

Mission

Our mission at QScript Coffee is to redefine the coffee experience. We strive to set new standards of quality and sustainability in the industry, ensuring that every cup reflects our commitment to excellence. Through community engagement, education, and a relentless pursuit of perfection, we aim to inspire a love for coffee that goes beyond taste, fostering a culture of appreciation, connection, and discovery.

MENU

COFFEE

  • Espresso: A single shot of intense and flavorsome coffee made by forcing hot water through finely ground coffee beans.
  • Doppio: A double shot of espresso, resulting in 60 ml of coffee, without added hot water or milk, providing a strong and rich flavor.
  • Ristretto: A concentrated shot of espresso made with half the amount of water, offering a delicious and intense coffee experience.
  • Long Black: A double-shot of espresso poured over hot water, retaining the crema and delivering a strong, flavorful coffee without dilution.
  • Short Macchiato: An espresso shot topped with creamy steamed milk, providing a balanced and slightly creamy coffee experience.
  • Long Macchiato: Similar to a short macchiato but with a double shot of coffee, offering a more robust flavor profile.
  • Mezzo Mezzo (Piccolo): A single espresso shot in a small latte glass filled with steamed milk, creating a harmonious blend of coffee and milk.
  • Cappuccino: A classic coffee drink consisting of one part espresso, one part textured milk, and one part froth, often topped with chocolate for a finishing touch.
